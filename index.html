About

Hey, Thanks for visiting my blog! This is the third website I've published, and my favorite one so far.

You see, before this website I had one site dedicated to Sports Economics, and another dedicated to Personal Finance. Knowing that I have a tendency to pick-up and put-down new interests swiftly, I created the websites hoping to catalogue and encourage my growth and commitment in both domains. This strategy started off great, but before too long, I was hit with a familiar wave of boredom in my former interests. The sites sat idle for about 2 months before I got the urge to begin coding again.

Jack Shea's Blog is the augmentation of my two former websites with my daily personal goals. It merges my day-to-day with my long-term, and allows me to finally catalogue the exploration of my interests. Instead of being pigeon-holed into writing only about sports economics, or personal finance, I'm able to write/code/post whatever I want. I don't know what my lasting interests will be, but I do know that I want to systematically explore them. Jack Shea's Blog is that exploration.

Thanks again!
